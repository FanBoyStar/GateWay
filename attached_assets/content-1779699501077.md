### What is a crypto credit card and how does it work?

A crypto credit card lets you spend your cryptocurrency at any merchant that accepts regular cards. You load it with crypto like SOL or USDC, and when you tap or swipe, it converts to local currency instantly at the point of sale. No merchant needs to accept crypto directly — it just works like any credit card.
